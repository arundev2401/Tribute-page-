📖 About

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers in the world. Known for his aggressive style, consistency, and unmatched passion, he has redefined modern cricket. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, he quickly rose through the ranks to become the captain of the Indian cricket team.

🏆 Achievements

  • Former Captain of Indian National Team
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times
  • Fastest to 8000, 9000, 10000 ODI runs
  • One of the highest century scorers in international cricket
  • Led India to historic Test series wins
